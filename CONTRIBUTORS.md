## Credits — original game designers

Several included environments are clean-room implementations of rulesets originally designed by others. Credit where due:

- **Coup** — designed by **Rikki Tahta**. Published by La Mame Games / Indie Boards & Cards. This repo's implementation is clean-room and ships no copyrighted card text or art; trademark and design credit remain with the author and publisher.
- **Vibe Check** — inspired by the spectrum-guessing mechanic from ***Wavelength*** by **Wolfgang Warsch, Alex Hague, and Justin Vickers** (published by CMYK). This repo's implementation is clean-room; trademark and design credit remain with the authors and publisher.
- **Wordle** — guess-feedback mechanic popularised by Josh Wardle's *Wordle*, now operated by The New York Times. This repo ships a multi-player variant using publicly-distributed word lists; no NYT assets or trademarks.
- **Poker** (Heads-Up No-Limit Texas Hold'em) — public-domain rules.
- **Tic-Tac-Toe** — public-domain rules.
- **Connect Four** — name is a registered trademark of Hasbro. The rules for "four-in-a-row with gravity" are public-domain. This implementation is clean-room and ships no Hasbro art, text, or branding.
- **Red Button** — original mechanic implemented in this repo.

No copyrighted rulebook text, card text, or game art from the above is shipped here. If you believe an environment misrepresents or infringes on a copyrighted work, please open an issue.
